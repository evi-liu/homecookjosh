Classic Chicken Soup

45 minutes • Serves 6

Ingredients

  • 2 whole chicken breasts
  • 3 carrots, chopped
  • 3 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, bring chicken broth to a boil.
  2. Add chicken breasts and simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. Remove chicken, shred it, and set aside.
  4. Add vegetables and bay leaves to the broth.
  5. Simmer for 15 minutes until vegetables are tender.
  6. Return shredded chicken to the pot.
  7. Season with salt and pepper.
  8. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
