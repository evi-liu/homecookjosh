RECIPE

CHAR SIU

Our recipe for Chinese BBQ pork that's perfectly cooked—with a sweet-savory glaze and tender, juicy meat inside.

by: The Woks of Life
SERVES: 6
⏱ PREP: 5 minutes
COOK: 15 minutes
TOTAL: 20 minutes

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 pounds pork shoulder/pork butt (cut into long strips)
  • ¼ cup hoisin sauce
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • ¼ cup Chinese rice wine
  • ⅓ cup honey
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tablespoons five spice powder
  • 1 tablespoon white pepper
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Cut the pork into long strips or chunks about 3 inches thick. Place in a large bowl.
  2. Combine all marinade ingredients in a bowl and mix well. Pour over the pork and massage into the meat.
  3. Cover and refrigerate overnight, or at least 8 hours.
  4. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a roasting pan with foil and place a wire rack on top.
  5. Place the pork on the rack, reserving the marinade. Roast for 25 minutes.
